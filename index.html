Common Questions
Frequently Asked Questions
What is your product design process?
My design workflow spans user research, user journey mapping, wireframing, high-fidelity UI design in Figma, clickable prototyping, usability testing, and systematic developer handoff. I follow user-centered principles to create accessible, logical, and highly converting digital products.
Do you work on an hourly rate or fixed project basis?
I support multiple collaboration models. For well-scoped projects with clear specifications, I operate on a fixed-bid basis. For ongoing product growth, iterative additions, or design system management, I work on a monthly or weekly retainer.
Which design tools do you use?
Figma is my primary tool for visual layout design, components, library creation, and prototyping. I also use FigJam/Miro for structural flows and user research synthesis, Maze for quantitative usability tests, and Adobe Creative Suite for visual design assets.
Can you collaborate directly with our engineering team?
Absolutely. I believe design is only as good as the final code. I structure my Figma files using native auto-layouts, clean variables/tokens, and clear states. I host review sessions, address engineering constraints, and ensure a seamless developer handoff.
